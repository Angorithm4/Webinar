Digital Modulation

Target:

Make the signal suitable for transmission in channel.

Usual Methods:

Digital Data

 ASK (Amplitude-Shift Keying);  FSK (Frequency-Shift Keying);  PSK (Phase-Shift Keying);

Analog Data

 AM (Amplitude Modulation);  PM (Phase Modulation);  FM (Frequency Modulation);

1. 2ASK

 Spread digital signal through the amplitude of a carrier wave, or on-off-keying.

  • Modulate :
      1. Analog Modulation.
      1. Keying

  • Demodulate :
      1. Noncoherent.

2. 2FSK

 Spread digital data through the frequency of a carrier wave.

  • Modulate:
    • Keying
  • Demodulate :

3. 2PSK

 Spread digital signal through the phase of a carrier wave.

  • Modulate :

      1. Analog Modulation.
      1. Keying.

  • Demodulate :

    • Coherent
